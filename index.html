Delicious Pancake Recipe

Hello, let's make delicious pancakes in 30 minutes

ingredients list

Serves 5 people

  • 1 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 table spoons of sugar
  • 1 tea spoon of salt
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1/2 cup of oil

instructions

  1. In a bowl mix flour, sugar and salt
  2. Add the eggs
  3. Add hot (not boiling) milk
  4. Mix until it has a smooth and medium consistency
  5. In a hot pan add oil and spread it
  6. Add the mixture and spread
  7. Flip the pancake after a minute
  8. Let cook the other side for 30 secs
  9. put it on a plate and do this steps for the rest

Enjoy with a cup of tea and syrup.
